The White Siberian Tiger

The White Siberian Tiger is one of biggest cat-animal in the world.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Tigris Altaica
  • Average Lenght: 195 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 18 years
  • Habitat: Korean Pine broadleaf forests

The White Siberian Tiger is reddish-rusty, or rusty-white in color, with narrow black transverse stripes. The body length is not less than 150 cm. The skull of the white siberian tiger is characterized by its large size and is similar to the skull of a lion.
